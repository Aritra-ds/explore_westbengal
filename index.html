Welcome to Bengal

West Bengal is a land of cultural richness and natural beauty. From the Himalayas in the north to the mangroves of the Sunderbans in the south, it’s a state of contrast and vibrancy.

History of West Bengal

West Bengal has a deep-rooted history that dates back to ancient times. It was once part of the Maurya and Gupta empires, and later became a center of British colonial power. Its capital, Kolkata, was formerly the capital of British India.
