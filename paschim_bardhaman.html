Explore Paschim Bardhaman

A land of rich industrial heritage and scenic beauty.

History of Paschim Bardhaman

Paschim Bardhaman is known for its industrial significance, particularly its coal mines, steel plants, and other manufacturing industries. The district has witnessed rapid development in the past few decades and has contributed significantly to the state's economy.

With its strategic location and historical landmarks, Paschim Bardhaman is a blend of modern industry and rich cultural heritage. The district is also home to several important temples and historical monuments that reflect its deep-rooted legacy.

Tourist Attractions

Burnpur Steel Plant – An iconic industrial hub and symbol of development.

Maa Kalyaneshwari Temple – A sacred shrine of Goddess Kalyaneshwari.

Deul Park – A peaceful park with scenic beauty, perfect for picnics.

Anand Amusement Park – A fun spot with exciting rides for all ages.

Spandan Recreation Park – A lively park with activities and serene green spaces.

Cultural Heritage

Paschim Bardhaman celebrates a rich blend of traditional festivals and cultural activities, especially during Durga Puja.

Art & Craft

The district is known for its folk art, including terracotta works, and unique handlooms.

Heritage Sites

The district is home to various historical landmarks, temples, and colonial-era structures that narrate its glorious past.
